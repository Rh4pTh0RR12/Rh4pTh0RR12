Privacy Policy for Askerden Studio Games

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Metris ("Application"), created by Askerden Studio. The Application is a falling sand block puzzle game designed for mobile devices.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect or store any personal identification information (such as your name, address, email address, or phone number). The Application only utilizes local storage to save game high scores, game states, and user preferences locally on your device.

Third-Party Services & Advertising

The Application uses Google AdMob to deliver advertisements. AdMob may collect and use advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) and non-personal diagnostic data to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements, track ad performance, and prevent fraud. You can learn more about AdMob's data collection policies by visiting: Google Privacy & Terms.

Device Permissions

The Application may request permissions to access device vibration motors for haptic feedback during gameplay. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contact list, or photos.

Children's Privacy

Our Application does not target and is not structured to attract children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: askerdenstudio@gmail.com
